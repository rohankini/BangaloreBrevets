Raise a big toast to Bangalore as we go international! The BumsOnTheSaddle community along with Cleated Warriors team worked with the organisers of the Paris - Brest-Paris (PBP) to put Bangalore on the world brevet map - a great achievement indeed! To participate in PBP 2011 (a once in a 4 year event) one has to complete the qualifying events held in France or in affiliated clubs across the world. These qualifying events are called brevet rides. There are 4 brevet rides that one has to ride and complete within a time frame. These rides can also be used by riders in other cities (and countries) to complete their qualifications for the main event. We have thrown in an extra 1000k ride at the end, just to make sure you are really prepared!
Get ready for the ultimate challenge of the brevets coming to a road near you in 2011 - they are going to be really tough - but we know many of you will rise up to the challenge! The distances and dates for Bangalore are:

Distance Date
200 kms January 15, 2011 
300 kms February 12, 2011
400 kms March 12, 2011
600 kms Apri 15, 2011
1000 kms June 24, 2011

Mark your calendars ! An opening launch ride for these brevets is planned for Dec 5, 2010 on Bellary Road
