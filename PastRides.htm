Some testimonials:

“What an awesome ride today!! Starting with 100+ riders, flooding the streets with colourful jerseys, facing the menacing headwinds, breakfast at Kamat Upachar, more headwinds, and endless climbs while returning. One of my best rides till date. Got every ounce of my confidence back. ~200kms/7hr 20mins.. Big thanks to volunteers and all the riders for whom this of the first 200 or even 100.” - Akshay Prabhu Gaonkar

“Thank you all for the opportunity. It was my best ride as well. I had missed to thank the volunteers on my way out of Cubbon Park. Thank you all of you guys.” - Shankar Ramachandran

“ Congrats to all those who made it happen yesterday. Organisers, participants, Team Veloscope. You guys are fantastic.” - Barani
