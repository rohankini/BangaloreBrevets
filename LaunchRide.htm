Launch Ride

 Bangalore Brevets will launch with an inaugural 200km ride on December 5th, 2010! This ride is not ACP sanctioned, but a teaser for the actual 200km ride to come in January.

This launch ride also has the option of riding 100km. Just show up at the registration desk and select the same on your brevet card.

Live Updates

Please check the Bangalore Brevets Facebook page for live updates, you do not need a Facebook account for this.

Route

This is a only a draft.

Instructions

Please be there at Cubbon park, opposite to Jewels de Paragon at 5.30 am onwards. Brevets to start at 6.30 am. Collect your brevet card at the registration desk.

Important Rules

See the ride rules for the complete set of rules, here are the most important ones:
  1. Reflective vest / strips (you get these on SP road, and near Corporation Circle)
  2. Helmets
  3. Tail light and front/head lights
