Raise a big toast to Bangalore as we go international! To participate in PBP 2011 (a once in a 4 year event) one has to complete the qualifying events held in France or in affiliated clubs across the world. These qualifying events are called brevet rides. There are 4 brevet rides that one has to ride and complete within a time frame. These rides can also be used by riders in other cities (and countries) to complete their qualifications for the main event. We have thrown in an extra 1000k ride at the end, just to make sure you are really prepared! Register for Brevet 200K 15th Jan 2011... Last date 08th Jan 2011... 77 Riders have registered so far...

The route

Start from Cubbon Park, ride 100kms towards Mandya on Mysore Road and then return 100kms.

Distance (from  start) Place
0 Cubbon Park, 06.00 am, opposite Jewels de Paragaon.
100 Mandya
200 Cubbon Park, 07.30 am, opposite Jewels de Paragaon.

Instructions

Please be there at Cubbon park, opposite to Jewels de Paragon at 5.30 am onwards. Brevets to start at 6.00 am. Collect your brevet card at the registration desk.

Make sure you've gone through the Checklist.

Important Rules

See the ride rules for the complete set of rules, here are the most important ones:
  1. Reflective vest / strips (you get these on SP road, and near Corporation Circle)
  2. Helmets
  3. Tail light and front/head lights
