Bangalore Brevets trial launch ride sees a great turnout!

December 5, 2010

On a still dark and chilly Bangalore morning, a sea of bikers in colourful attire and bikes, descended at Cubbon Park to kick off the inaugural brevet, month end long ride and the Go Green ride on December 5, 2010. With the reflective vests (which will be compulsory for the actual brevet ride) that shone brightly in the dark, it was indeed a sight to behold!

The ride was flagged off at around 7 am and the count indicated nearly 120 bikers who turned up for different distances. It turned out to be a rather pleasant and overcast morning and bikers enjoyed the cool weather for quite a few hours and the sun didn’t make an appearance. Volunteers at different time stations were all ready to receive the bikers as they trickled in.

Like every ride there were punctures, bike problems but did that not deter folks from logging in anywhere between 50, 100 and of course, 200 kms. At the last count, the team recorded 53 bikers who registered at the 150km time station and 52 at the 200km time station. There many more riders who did the 50k distance too.

Bellary Road (aka New Airport Road) was flooded with cycles and Kamat did roaring business as hungry bikers trooped in for much needed nutrition.

The route was fairly simple – out and back on Bellary road but it turned out to be quite a challenge with the headwinds on the way and the rolling terrain (which you don’t realize till you’re actually on non-motorized wheels!).

The official time for finishing the race was 13.5 hours and there were still folks who came in at around 7 pm in the evening to finish off their 200kms.

A big shout out to the volunteers who came out to help – Anil and Vishal and the team BOTs folks – Rohan and Vasu who played Race Director for the event.

We look forward to 2011 as the first Brevet ride will be kicked off on Jan 15. If you want to register as a volunteer or a participant, we’d love to hear from you!
